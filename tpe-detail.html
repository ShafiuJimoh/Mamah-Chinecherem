The People's Economy

Finance & Policy Analysis Platform

Project Overview

The People's Economy is a finance and policy analysis platform focused on making economic concepts accessible to everyday Nigerians. As the Social Media Manager, I built their Instagram presence from the ground up, creating a cohesive brand identity and implementing a strategic content calendar that resonated with their target audience.

Within 30 days, we achieved exceptional reach metrics with 138K+ views and reached over 41K accounts, with an impressive 93% non-follower reach rate demonstrating strong brand awareness beyond our existing audience.
