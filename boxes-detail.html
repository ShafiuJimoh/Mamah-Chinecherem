Project Overview

Boxes by PG is a luxury packaging brand specializing in premium gift boxes, custom packaging solutions, and elegant presentation materials. As the Content Creator & Social Media Manager, I developed and executed a comprehensive social media strategy that positioned the brand as a go-to choice for luxury packaging in Lagos.

My role encompassed all aspects of social media management, from photography and graphic design to content strategy and community engagement, resulting in consistent growth and strong brand recognition.
